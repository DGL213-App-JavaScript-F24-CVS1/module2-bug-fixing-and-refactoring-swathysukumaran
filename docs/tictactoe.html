Tic Tac Toe

A DGL 213 Game

How to play

Goal: Get three of your marks (X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

  • Players take turns to click on a cell to place their mark.
  • The first player to get three marks in a row wins!
  • Use the Undo button to revert your last move.
  • Use the Restart button to start a new game.
